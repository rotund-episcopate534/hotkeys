## 🎙️ Using speech to text

This feature turns your voice into text in real-time. Follow these steps to use it:

1. Click the icon in your system tray.
2. Select the "Start Dictation" option.
3. Speak clearly into your microphone.
4. Press the hotkey again to stop the recording. 

The software uses advanced processing to convert your speech into accurate text. It recognizes most accents and common speech patterns. Ensure your microphone works correctly by checking your Windows sound settings if the app fails to hear you.
